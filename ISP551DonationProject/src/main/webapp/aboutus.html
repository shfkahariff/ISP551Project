ABOUT US

Surau Al Sakeenah of Taman Tun Dr Ismail Jaya of Shah Alam, Selangor was built in 2008 on piece land that was gazette by the developer TTDI Development Sdn Bhd.


Events are commonly hosted in surau, whether it is a local gathering or an annual Islamic festival such as Hari Raya Haji.


Surau community provides several services such as donation service, funeral service, community service such as visiting poor and asnaf, as well as a 'Muallaf' centre in conjunction with Hidayah Center Foundation.
